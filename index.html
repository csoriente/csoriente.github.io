About

I am a security and privacy specialist with extensive experience in research and innovation. I currently serve as Senior Product Manager and Lead Cryptographer at GMV Spain, where I work on topics including digital identity, quantum key management, and secure AI.

Before my current position, I worked at NEC Laboratories Europe and Telefónica Research. I received my PhD from the University of California, Irvine and I was a postdoctoral research at ETH Zurich. I have also been a visiting researcher at Inria Rhône-Alpes, IBM Research Zurich, and the IMDEA Software Institute. In addition, I teach part-time in the master’s program at Universidad Camilo José Cela.

My interests span secure computation, privacy-enhancing technologies, and authentication. I also remain active in research, publishing regularly in academic venues and serving on the technical program committees of leading security and privacy conferences.
